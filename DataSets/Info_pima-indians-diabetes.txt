Target: Label

Columns:

pregnant: Number of times the patient has been pregnant; an indicator of reproductive history, which may relate to diabetes risk.

glucose: Plasma glucose concentration after a 2-hour oral glucose tolerance test (mg/dL); measures blood sugar levels.

bp (blood pressure): Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg); an important cardiovascular health indicator.

skin: Triceps skinfold thickness (mm); used as a measure of body fat.

insulin: 2-hour serum insulin level (µU/mL); reflects insulin production and glucose regulation.

bmi (Body Mass Index): Weight-to-height ratio (kg/m²); measures body fat and obesity level.

pedigree: Diabetes pedigree function; quantifies the genetic likelihood of diabetes based on family history.

age: Age of the patient (in years); older age is associated with higher diabetes risk.

label: Outcome variable indicating diabetes status (1 = diabetic, 0 = non-diabetic).
